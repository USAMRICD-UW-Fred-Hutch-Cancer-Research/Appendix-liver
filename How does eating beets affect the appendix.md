* **Nutrient-Rich:** The juice of beets is considered a healthy source of nutrients that may help manage symptoms related to appendicitis, such as pain and bloating.  
* **Fiber and Digestion:** A cup of raw beets contains significant fiber ($\>15\\%$ of the daily value), which, if consumed in excess, might cause bloating or cramps.  
* **FODMAP Content:** Raw beets are high in fructans (a type of fermentable carbohydrate), which may cause digestive distress for some individuals.
